# Description
This is the frontend of Gogimail.

# Service
Gogimail handles your email marketing from the AI input, to the content creation and delivery.

# Fast & Convenient
Gogimail keeps your email marketing housed in one system and delivers your promotions to your customer list in only a few clicks!

# Safety
Gogimail's AI is trained to reduce hallucinations and optimize accuracy.
Human approval is incorporated to further prevent any liability as a result of incorrect AI outputs.
